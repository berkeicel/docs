---
title: "Core Concepts"
description: "How RampKit works"
---

1. **Design in the editor** — Create your onboarding in the RampKit dashboard
2. **Initialize the SDK** — Call `RampKit.init()` with your app ID
3. **Show onboarding** — Call `RampKit.showOnboarding()`
4. **Handle completion** — Use the `onOnboardingFinished` callback

Everything else—screens, navigation, animations, haptics, personalization—is configured in the editor.
