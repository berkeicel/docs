## Quick Setup

1. Install: `npx expo install rampkit-expo-dev react-native-webview react-native-pager-view react-native-root-siblings`
2. Init: `await RampKit.init({ appId: "YOUR_APP_ID" })`
3. Show: `RampKit.showOnboarding()`

Design your onboarding in the [RampKit dashboard](https://rampkit.com).
